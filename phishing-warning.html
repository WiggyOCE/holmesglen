Be Wary of Phishing Attempts!

This page was part of a controlled phishing simulation for educational purposes. No real information was collected.

Learn to Protect Yourself:

  • Verify the sender: Always check the sender’s email address and look for anything unusual.
  • Look for HTTPS: Ensure the URL starts with “https” and includes the lock icon.
  • Avoid urgent language: Phishing emails often use urgent language to make you act quickly.
  • Never click unknown links: Hover over links to verify the destination before clicking.
  • Contact the organisation directly: If unsure, reach out to the company through official channels.

Remember, your awareness is the best defence against phishing threats!
